About CupIT

I'm a coffee roaster. I needed a proper place to log my cuppings — something purpose-built, not a spreadsheet or a notebook that gets lost. So I built one. And once it existed, it felt wrong to keep it to myself.

CupIT is free and has no advertising.

If you have suggestions or improvements, reach out through the feedback form below. This is a community project and it only gets better with community input.

Our philosophy

"Because taste is subjective and scores create bias, you will find no scoring on this site."
