The idea

A cupping journal that
actually works

Most cuppers rely on notebooks, spreadsheets, or just memory. That means lost notes, forgotten coffees, and no real way to see how your palate is developing over time.

CupIT is built to fix that. One place to log every session — the coffee, the roaster, the flavour notes, the score. Organised, searchable, and always with you.

"What did I think of that Ethiopian natural six months ago? Now you can actually find out."

No spreadsheets. No lost notebooks. Just a clean, simple journal that grows with your palate.

Built around
how cuppers actually work

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

01 📓

Log every cupping

Record the coffee, roaster, origin, process and date. Add your flavour notes and a score. Done in under a minute.

02 🍓

Pick your flavours

Our flavour wheel makes it quick and consistent. Tap families, narrow it down, or keep it broad — your call every time.

03 ⚖️

Score your coffee

Rate the attributes that matter to you — acidity, body, sweetness, finish. Build a consistent scoring habit over time.

04 📊

Watch your palate grow

See patterns emerge across your sessions. Which origins do you gravitate to? What processing styles suit your palate?

05 🔍

Search and revisit

Find any cupping in seconds. Search by coffee name, roaster, flavour note or score. Your history, always at hand.

06 ☕

Compare side by side

Pull two cuppings together and compare them directly. Useful when you're deciding what to reorder — or just curious.
